## Demo Flow

1. Open the live website.
2. Select an image.
3. Preview the selected image.
4. Click Analyze image.
5. Read similarity, duplicate status, owner, and blockchain status.

## Judge Pitch

SecureMedia AI helps protect originality and improve trust in digital media. It combines image hashing, optional AI similarity, and blockchain-ready ownership tracking in one simple upload workflow.
